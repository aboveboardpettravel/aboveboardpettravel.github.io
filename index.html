About

Above Board Pet Travel is a full-service pet relocation company based in Fethiye, Turkey. We routinely travel between Turkey and the UK.

We are here to provide your pet the true VIP travel experience!

We are pet owners too, so we understand the importance of providing exceptional service and value at a fair and competitive rate.

We love animals, and the comfort and safety of every pet is our number one priority.

We understand how difficult it can be to be separated from your pet, so we know how important constant communication is.

We love what we do, and we understand how important your pets are to you. We treat your pets as if they are our own!

THE PROCESS

It will take 120 days to get your pet ready for their travel to the UK. We can make all arrangements including vet trips 1. Microchip 2. Rabies Vaccination (the UK does honour the 3 year vaccination 3. Rabies Titer Test, 30 days after the rabies vaccination 4. Tapeworm Treatment (dogs only) 5 days prior to travel 5. Health Certificate from registered vet

TRANSPORT

Our vehicle is fully DEFRA compliant with a Type 2 Animal Transport Certificate and has EU Certificate of Approval Our vehicle has a maximum of 15 cages, it is fully air conditioned for summer and heated in winter. In each cage a bed and water is provided. A litter tray is provided for cats. Your pets are fed a high quality pet food supplied by us unless otherwise specified. We have regular stops to feed and walk your pet. Every overnight stop at least 1 person sleeps in the vehicle with your pet.

FINANCIAL

Once you have decided to use Above Board Pet Travel, you can pay in monthly instalments if required to help spread the cost, as long as the full balance is paid by the travel date.
